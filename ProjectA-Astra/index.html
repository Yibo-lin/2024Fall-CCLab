[ Astra ]

Scientific Name:

Astra

Discovered by / at:

Lindsey, 09/25/2024, 19:32 UTC+8

Family:

According to Aquaflora, Astra used to be part of the sky creature community. Its family members can fly but cannot swim while Astra behaves very differently due to gene mutation and eventually finds itself comfortable living underwater, which is one of a kind. Its family are also playful, curious creatures, often forming small, tight-knit communities. The Astra family remain elusive, with many of their behaviors still shrouded in mystery.

Habitat:

In Ocean: Active and playful. It tends to hide very well among coral reefs during the night. On Land: Fearful and timid. Hiding itself among bushes and doesn't want to be noticed.

Appearance:

Astra is a fish-like creature with a thin, long body. Unlike an eel, it has a fan-shaped tail covered with scales. It has no wings or feet, only two small fins. Though it is said to have colorful scales, it can't be observed during the day. Therefore, it remains a mystery whether it has beautiful scales for human beings to observe.

Key Characteristics:

  • Playful in water
  • Sensitive on land
  • Fan-shaped tail
  • Hides in bushes and coral reefs
  • Feeds on small fish and algae

What we know so far about [ Astra ]? The origin of its name comes from 'Aquaflora', an ancient myth of an tribe in the Atlantic Ocean islands. It is said that the creature gets this name because it has iridescent scales that shimmers with blue, navy, and silver, mimicking the ocean's colors. However, there's yet no any photos or videos to prove this appearance. In Ocean, Astra can do wonderful things in watery environments. Its distinctive scales reflect lights in water and made the water around it glowing with beautiful colors. Astra is playful and sociable underwater. The water covering its body and scales makes it feel protected and creates current to make Astra move more smoothly in water. Astra rests among coral reefs, making disguise using its shiny scales. It feeds on algae, small fish, and aquatic insects. It is afraid of jellyfish undersea, for their tectacles release toxic liquid that could burn the scales of Astra. On Land, due to the unqiue and delicate scales of Astra, it is very sensitive to light and even dust in the air. Astra's special body structure and acoustic detecting abilities makes it feel too much noise and pressure on land. When the water environment is not suitable to live in, it rapidly goes into bushes to make disguise and to avoid predators or attention from human beings.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Astra is shy in general. Therefore, it is better to observe different behaviors of Astra under different living conditions or faced with bait or enemy, instead of directly interacting with it. The observer can change the water level of the lake (tap and hold water). When water level is low, it jumps into the bushes. Observers can send out a cat to approach Astra in the bushes. When Astra is swimming in the water, observer can add bait or enemy to the lake(press the happy/sad face emoji). Astra's swimming speed, direction, swimming patterns will change accordingly.
